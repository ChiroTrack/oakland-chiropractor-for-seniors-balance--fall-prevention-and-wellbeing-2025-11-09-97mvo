Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Yes, chiropractic care is very safe for seniors when provided by qualified practitioners who specialize in senior care. At The Shift Oakland Chiropractor, we use gentle, evidence-based techniques specifically designed for older adults. We thoroughly assess your health history, current conditions, and medications before beginning treatment. Our chiropractors are trained in age-appropriate adjustments and modifications that respect the unique needs of aging bodies. We coordinate with your other healthcare providers to ensure safe, comprehensive care. If you have any specific health concerns, we encourage you to discuss them with us during your initial consultation.

Falls are often caused by poor balance, weak core muscles, and spinal misalignment. Our fall prevention program addresses these issues through several approaches: spinal adjustments improve alignment and nerve function, which enhances balance and proprioception; specific exercises strengthen your core and leg muscles; and postural corrections reduce strain on your joints. We also provide guidance on home safety and movement techniques. Many of our patients report significantly improved balance and confidence within just a few weeks of starting our fall prevention program. We'll work with you to develop a personalized plan based on your specific risk factors and health goals.

During your first visit, we'll spend time getting to know you and understanding your health history, current concerns, and goals. We'll perform a thorough physical examination, which may include orthopedic and neurological tests. Depending on your condition, we may take X-rays or other diagnostic images. We'll explain our findings in clear, easy-to-understand terms and discuss treatment options with you. Your first appointment typically lasts 45-60 minutes, and subsequent visits are usually 20-30 minutes. We encourage you to ask questions and share any concerns you may have. Our goal is to make you feel comfortable and confident in your care plan before we begin any treatment.

The frequency of your visits depends on your specific condition, the severity of your symptoms, and your overall health goals. Some patients benefit from twice-weekly visits initially, while others do well with weekly appointments. As you improve, we typically reduce the frequency to maintenance visits, which might be monthly or quarterly. We'll work with you to develop a treatment schedule that fits your needs and budget. Most patients notice significant improvement within 4-6 weeks of consistent treatment. We believe in efficient care, so we'll regularly reassess your progress and adjust your treatment plan as needed to ensure you're getting the maximum benefit from your visits.
